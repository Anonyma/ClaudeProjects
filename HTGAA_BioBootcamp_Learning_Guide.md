## ðŸ§ª Day 2: Research Methods & Wet Lab Skills

*Focus: How to design experiments and manipulate DNA in the lab.*

### 1. Key Concepts
*   **Literature & AI Research:**
    *   Using databases (PubMed, Google Scholar) and AI tools for effective literature reviews.
*   **Experimental Design:**
    *   **Controls:** The importance of Positive and Negative controls.
    *   **Reproducibility:** Maintaining a rigorous Lab Notebook.
*   **Molecular Cloning (The "Bread and Butter"):**
    *   **Restriction Enzyme Assembly:** Using molecular scissors and glue (Ligation).
    *   **Gibson Assembly:** A more modern, seamless way to join multiple DNA fragments.
    *   **PCR:** Exponentially amplifying specific DNA sequences.
    *   **Transformation:** "Shocking" DNA into bacteria to make them express your genes.

### 2. Lab Techniques & Visualization
*   **Gel Electrophoresis:** Verifying your DNA fragments by size.
*   **Scientific Figures:** Best practices for creating clear, data-driven visualizations.
