**Q4:** B - The Bessemer process allowed for the efficient production of steel by blowing air through molten iron to remove excess carbon.

**Q6:** B - Ammonia is crucial for synthetic fertilizers, which are vital for feeding the global population.

**Q7:** B - An organic economy is one that relies on recent solar energy, limiting what humanity could achieve with available materials.

**Q8:** C - Silicon is called 'the brain of the modern world' due to its essential role in electronics and computing.

**Q9:** B - Iron was difficult to produce and too brittle, which limited its use in construction and other applications.
