**Q5:** A - Baumol's cost disease refers to the situation where wages increase in sectors that do not experience productivity gains, leading to higher costs for services.

**Q6:** B - The podcast mentions Universal Basic Services as a potential alternative to UBI, focusing on providing essential services rather than cash payments.

**Q7:** B - The podcast argues that taxing robots would penalize productivity and efficiency, which could ultimately harm the economy.

**Q8:** B - The podcast emphasizes that UBI can provide a psychological safety net, reducing stress and allowing individuals to focus on improving their lives.

**Q9:** A - Techno-feudalism describes a scenario where a small group of tech elites control wealth and resources, leading to a lack of agency for the general population.
